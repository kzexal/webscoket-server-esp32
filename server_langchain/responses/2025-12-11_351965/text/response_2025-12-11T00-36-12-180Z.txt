Of course! Cooking rice is pretty simple. Here's a quickguide:

First, wash the rice to remove any dirt or impurities. Then, measure the rice you need and pour it intoa pot. Add water in a ratio of about 1:2, depending on how fluffy you like it. Bring it toa boil, then let it simmer for about 15-20minutes. Once it's cooked, drain any excess water, andyour rice is ready! You can fluff it up with afork before serving.
