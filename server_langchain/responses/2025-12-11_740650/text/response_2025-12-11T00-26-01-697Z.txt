Of course! Cooking rice is pretty simple. Here's a basicguide:

First, wash the rice until the water runs clear.Then, measure the rice and water in a ratio of 1:1.5 (e.g., 1 cup of ricefor 1.5 cups of water). Bring it to aboil, then reduce to a simmer. Let it cook for about20 minutes until all the water is absorbed. Fluff itwith a fork, and it's ready! Remember, the ricecan be served loose or stickier, depending on how long youcook it.
