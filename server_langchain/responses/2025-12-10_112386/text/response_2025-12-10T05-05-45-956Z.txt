That's great! Cooking rice is pretty simple. Here's aquick guide:

First, wash the rice until the water runs clear. Then, measure the rice and water in a ratio of1:1.5 (for example, 1 cup ofrice to 1.5 cups of water). Bring it toa boil, then let it simmer for about 15-20minutes, depending on the type of rice.

When it's ready, the rice should be fluffy and easy to flake. Letit rest for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your cooking!If you need more details, feel free to ask.
