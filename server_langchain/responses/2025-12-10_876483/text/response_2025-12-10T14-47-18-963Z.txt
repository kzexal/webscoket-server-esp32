Of course! Cooking rice is pretty simple. Here's a quickguide:

First, wash the rice to remove any dirt or impurities. Then, measure the rice you want to cook and rinseit a couple of times. Use one cup of rice per personyou're serving.

Next, bring water to a boil in apot. Once it's boiling, add the rice and stir.Let it cook for about 15-20 minutes until all thewater is absorbed.

Finally, remove from heat, let it restfor a few minutes, and fluff it with a fork.There you go, ready to serve!
